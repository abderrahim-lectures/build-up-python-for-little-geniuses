\section{Taking care of you}

A few honest notes before you start:

\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{Mistakes are not yet, not wrong.} Every ``bug'' in your code
    is a normal part of programming, even for adults who do this every day.
  \item \textbf{There are no streaks to keep here.} This book will never
    ask you to come back every day or make you feel bad for taking a break.
  \item \textbf{Take real breaks.} Chapters end with a good stopping point
    on purpose. Stretch, look away from the screen, and come back later.
  \item \textbf{Chapter 8 needs a grown-up.} That is when you install
    Python on your own computer for the first time. It is a good chapter to
    do together with a parent or guardian.
\end{itemize}
